ABOUT ME

I received my bachelors degree at the University of Wisconsin Stevens Point where I studied both theater and sociology. My experience in both of these areas has helped me be a designer with a strong sense of empathy as well as obtain experience working in a collaborative creative setting.

As a designer, I am passionate about creating positive experiences by finding creative solutions with attention to detail and empathy.
